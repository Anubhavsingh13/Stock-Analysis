## Executive Summary

Chemcon is a genuinely scarce business — India's only HMDS manufacturer and the world's largest CMIC producer — with a structurally intact moat that has been temporarily overwhelmed by Chinese pricing pressure and post-COVID pharma destocking. The balance sheet is clean, promoter pledge is zero, FCF has turned positive, and a semiconductor-driven HMDS demand upcycle creates a credible 3-year thesis. However, the stock at P/E 30x on trough earnings and 1.5x book already prices in near-full recovery, leaving insufficient margin of safety — buy below ₹185 or wait for a confirmed Stage 2 breakout above ₹215 with evidence of margin normalization.

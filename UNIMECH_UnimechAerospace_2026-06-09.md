## Executive Summary

Unimech Aerospace and Manufacturing Limited (NSE: UNIMECH, BSE: 544210) is a Bengaluru-based precision engineering company that manufactures complex aerospace tooling (aero engine tools, airframe tools) and precision components for global OEMs including Airbus, Boeing, GE, Rolls-Royce, and Dassault Aviation. The core investment thesis rests on a differentiated export-led model serving a structurally growing global aerospace MRO/tooling market, exceptional historical ROIC (54%+ in FY24), and a management team with deep OEM relationships built over 30+ years. The key risk is an abrupt FY26 revenue stagnation (₹240 Cr vs ₹243 Cr in FY25) combined with a massive working capital blow-up, a large acquisition (Hobel Bellows for ₹450 Cr — nearly 2x annual revenue) funded from IPO proceeds, and a valuation at ~74–92x P/E that prices in near-perfect execution with insufficient margin of safety at the current price of ~₹928.

## Executive Summary
Vaibhav Global is an omnichannel e-tailer of fashion jewellery and accessories with reach to ~127 million households via TV home shopping (Shop LC US, Shop TJC + Ideal World UK, Shop LC Germany) and digital platforms. The business has genuine quality hallmarks: ROIC 26.8% (10 years above WACC), WTT score 0.86 (high management credibility), zero promoter pledge, founder-led (Sunil Agrawal, 57.43%), FCF-backed earnings (102% conversion, ₹272 Cr FCF FY26). FY26: Revenue ₹3,692 Cr (+9.2%), EBITDA margin 10.8% (+140 bps), PAT ₹266 Cr (+74%). FY27 guidance: 9–11% revenue growth, 50–100 bps EBITDA expansion. Digital reached 44% of B2C (target 50% FY27); in-house brands hit 49% one year ahead of target. **At ₹255, MoS is inadequate (27% vs. 30–40% required) and upside/downside is 1.1:1 (below 2:1 minimum). Technical Stage 3. Buy zone: ₹200–215.**

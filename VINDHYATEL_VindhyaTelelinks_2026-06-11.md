## Executive Summary
Vindhya Telelinks is the M.P. Birla Group's cables-and-EPC flagship, operating in two segments: Cable Manufacturing (~30% revenue) and Engineering, Procurement & Construction (~70% revenue). FY26 consolidated revenue was ₹3,593 cr with consolidated PAT of ₹163 cr (14.5× P/E — genuinely cheap). However, the standalone business collapsed — standalone PAT fell 54% as finance costs surged, Q3 FY26 reported a ₹19.66 cr standalone loss, debtors ballooned to ₹2,192 cr (223 days), and CARE downgraded the company to CARE A/A1 with a Rating Watch. The underlying problem is singular: UP Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM) project disbursement delays have locked ~₹700+ cr in government receivables. The stock has already surged 60% in the past month post-Q4 FY26 results — the expected return at ₹1,932 is flat to modest. **Wait for a 15–20% pullback to ₹1,400–1,600 for an adequate margin of safety. VTL is a better vehicle than BIRLACABLE for the M.P. Birla cables thesis.**

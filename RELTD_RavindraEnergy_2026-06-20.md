# Stock Analysis Report — Ravindra Energy Ltd (NSE: RELTD)
**Date:** 2026-06-20 | **Recommendation:** AVOID | **Conviction Score:** 2 / 10
**CMP:** ~₹171

---

## Business Primer

Ravindra Energy Ltd is a solar IPP (Independent Power Producer) and EV associate company with 228.9 MWp commissioned and an ambitious 486 MWp total portfolio. The company operates in the PM-KUSUM and MSKVY Maharashtra solar schemes. The sector has genuine structural tailwinds (PM-KUSUM 2.0, India targeting 500 GW solar by 2030); however, this specific company is a hard avoid on multiple grounds.

---

## The Three Decisive Negatives

### 1. Governance Hard Veto (Fisher Point 15 = FAIL — AUTOMATIC VETO)

The promoter (Khandepar Investments / Narendra Murkumbi) pledged ₹111 Cr of shares in June 2026 — **the same month** they asked minority shareholders to subscribe to a ₹205 Cr rights issue — while simultaneously **renouncing their own ₹46.14 Cr rights entitlement**. This is textbook promoter extraction behavior:

- Asking minorities to put in fresh capital
- While promoter extracts by pledging existing shares
- While refusing to participate in their own rights issue

Combined with ₹353 Cr of unexplained losses in FY18–FY19, this triggers an **automatic veto under the skill's management integrity framework.**

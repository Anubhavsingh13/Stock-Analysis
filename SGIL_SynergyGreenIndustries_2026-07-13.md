## Executive Summary
Synergy Green is a Kolhapur-based iron/steel **casting foundry** supplying wind-turbine structural castings to global OEMs (Vestas >1/3 of the order book, GE, others), riding a genuine **China+1 + wind-energy secular tailwind**. However, a debt-funded brownfield expansion (30,000 → 45,000 TPA foundry + 20,000 TPA machining) has **tripled borrowings to ~₹250 Cr and pushed Net Debt/EBITDA to ~5×, ICR below 2×, and FY26 PAT down 72%**, while the stock trades at an extreme **188× trailing P/E, 8.5× book, and ~7× the Graham Number**. The thesis is a real business at a dangerous price: even flawless FY27–28 ramp execution leaves you paying ~35–40× forward earnings with distress-level leverage and single-customer concentration — no margin of safety and a poor downside asymmetry.

## Business Primer
Iron/steel castings (SG iron, grey iron, steel) up to 30 MT/piece for wind turbines — hubs, main frames, bearing housings — increasingly machined & coated in-house. Revenue = tonnes × realisation; ~80% wind energy, ~20% general engineering; ~25–30% exports. Part of the Shirgaokar Brothers / SB Reshellers group. Cyclical, asset-heavy, commodity-conversion manufacturer with a secular tailwind but thin margins, high leverage and extreme customer concentration.

## Executive Summary

Savita Oil Technologies (NSE: SOTL) is India's largest transformer oil manufacturer (~33% domestic market share) and Top-2 white oil supplier, operating as a near-debt-free specialty petroleum processor in the heart of India's power infrastructure buildout cycle. The business is forensically clean and well-governed (0% promoter pledge, E&Y internal auditor, no related-party concerns), and the stock is priced at a **pessimistic PIE** — the market implies only ~4% perpetual NOPAT growth vs. a sector growing at 7-8% volumes. However, at ₹479, the upside/downside ratio is barely 1:1 due to EBITDA margins of only 5-7% and significant commodity cyclicality (ROCE swung from 31% in FY22 to 10% in FY25). The key risk is that APAR Industries is becoming a formidable competitor. **Wait for ₹390-420 to buy; at current price, hold existing positions.**

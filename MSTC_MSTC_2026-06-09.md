## Executive Summary

MSTC Limited is a Miniratna Category-I PSU (Ministry of Steel) completing a decade-long pivot from capital-intensive scrap trading into India's dominant government e-auction and digital commodity exchange platform — with near-monopoly characteristics and 60%+ operating margins. The thesis rests on three new high-optionality platforms (EPR certificate trading exchange with a 15-year CPCB monopoly mandate, government travel portal via EaseMyTrip, machinery leasing) that could add ₹250–400 Cr in incremental annual revenue by FY29. The key risk is simultaneous execution of three nascent platforms against a backdrop of institutional exodus (FIIs -28%, MFs -83% YoY) and a Nifty 50 in Stage 3 decline.

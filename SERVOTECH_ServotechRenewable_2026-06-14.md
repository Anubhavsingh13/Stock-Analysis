## Executive Summary

Servotech Renewable Power System Ltd (NSE: SERVOTECH) is a domestic clean energy equipment manufacturer making solar inverters/batteries (51% of revenue), AC EV chargers (27%), and DC fast chargers (15%), with a growing retail distribution channel and a small but expanding export footprint via its Dubai subsidiary. The company operates in structurally attractive, government-policy-driven sectors (PM Surya Ghar Yojana, PM E-DRIVE) but has delivered three consecutive years of **negative operating cash flow**, seen its borrowings triple to ₹198Cr in a single year, and grown standalone revenue by only 8.9% in FY26 — a violent deceleration from 91% in FY25. At ₹103 (P/E 64x, EV/EBITDA 34x), the stock is priced for an immaculate bull case in a business with no durable moat, poor earnings quality, and an asymmetric downside of 59% (SVAR). **AVOID.**

## Module 9 — Valuation: PIE First, Then DCF

### 9A. Price-Implied Expectations (Mauboussin PIE)

```

PIE Implied Perpetual Growth:
g = (EV × WACC − NOPAT) / (EV + NOPAT)
= (2494 × 0.122 − 45.2) / (2494 + 45.2)
= 259.1 / 2539.2
= 10.2% perpetual NOPAT CAGR
```

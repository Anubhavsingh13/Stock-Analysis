The rare edtech survivor of the post-BYJU'S shakeout: scaled to ₹3,900 cr revenue (FY26, +35%) AND reached PAT breakeven (loss ₹24 cr) with EBITDA margin doubling 7%→14%. Genuinely impressive. BUT at ~9.6× sales / ~68× EV-EBITDA there's no margin of safety, the moat is young and key-man-dependent, and there's no public track record. Speculative growth, not a MoS investment.

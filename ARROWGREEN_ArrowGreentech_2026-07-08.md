## Executive Summary
Arrow Greentech is a debt-free, IP-rich (~32 patents) small-cap in security printing / anti-counterfeit (~90% of revenue) plus water-soluble PVA films (~10%) and a small pharma-strip subsidiary (Avery). The thesis blocker is **extreme earnings lumpiness** — three loss years in the last decade (FY18/19/20/22) and a FY23–25 order/patent-driven surge that has **already rolled over in FY26** (revenue −17%, PAT −25%). At ~22× reported / ~27× normalised earnings and ~2× Graham value on a *declining* trend, in a Stage 3/4 technical, there is no margin of safety. Real business, wrong price, poor visibility.

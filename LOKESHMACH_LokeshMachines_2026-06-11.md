## Executive Summary

Lokesh Machines Limited is a Hyderabad-based CNC machine tools and auto-component manufacturer that has been placed on the US OFAC Specially Designated Nationals (SDN) list since October 30, 2024, for allegedly supplying machine tools to Russian defence procurement networks — a hard stop that renders the company uninvestable until the sanction is removed. The company's fundamentals were already deteriorating pre-sanctions (revenue CAGR -4% over 3 years, ROCE <7%, negative FCF), and the OFAC listing caused a 22% revenue collapse in FY25. While FY26 shows a topline recovery driven primarily by the domestic order book, the unresolved geopolitical overhang, elevated debt (₹169 Cr), severely stretched working capital (inventory days ~680, debtor days ~121), and negligible returns on capital (ROE 0.82%, ROCE 3.09%) combine to make the current price of ~₹255 at P/E >140x unjustifiable for a 2–3 year investment horizon.

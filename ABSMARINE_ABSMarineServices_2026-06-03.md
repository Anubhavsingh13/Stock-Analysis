## Executive Summary

ABS Marine Services is a 30-year-old Chennai-based ship owner and manager pivoting from fee-based ship management to asset-heavy OSV ownership, riding India's ONGC offshore capex supercycle. The business delivered 75% revenue growth and 179% EBITDA growth in FY26, with a 47% EBITDA margin and ROIC of 21% — well above cost of capital. Trading at 8.7x P/E and ~6x EV/EBITDA, the stock is priced as if growth is over; the PIE analysis reveals the market is pricing in only 5% perpetual growth against a realistic 18–22% CAGR scenario. The primary risk is ONGC client concentration and the SME listing which caps institutional buying — the latter creates the opportunity. The single largest re-rating catalyst is NSE Mainboard migration (achievable by May 2027).

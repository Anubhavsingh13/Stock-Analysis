## Business Primer

GK Energy Limited is a Pune-based EPC (Engineering, Procurement & Commissioning) services company focused almost exclusively on solar-powered agricultural water pump systems under PM-KUSUM (Component B). Revenue flows as milestone-based project payments from state nodal agencies (primarily MSEDCL in Maharashtra). FY26 revenue: ₹1,533 crore; 276 MW commissioned; 61,085 pump systems installed.

The company is transitioning from an asset-light EPC contractor toward a capital-intensive manufacturer via a 1 GW solar panel manufacturing facility (~₹250 Cr debt-funded capex). This pivot is the central analytical question: does it create or destroy value?

**Key value drivers:** PM-KUSUM scheme continuity + solar module procurement cost (50–60% of project value)
**Key risk:** 83% revenue concentration in one government programme

## Business Primer

M&B Engineering Ltd (founded 1981 as Manibhai and Brothers Construction, Ahmedabad) is a Gujarat-headquartered manufacturer of pre-engineered steel buildings (PEB) and self-supported steel roofing systems. Two divisions: **Phenix** (PEB design, fabrication, erection — 78% of FY26 revenue, ₹985 Cr) and **Proflex** (proprietary self-supported roofing via 18 mobile fabrication units — 22% of revenue, ₹275 Cr). Plants at Sanand (Gujarat, 2008) and Cheyyar (Tamil Nadu, FY24) with combined PEB capacity 103,800 MTPA. The Sanand plant holds India's only dual AISC (US) and CWB (Canada) certification — enabling export of certified PEB to North America at 16–17% EBITDA margin vs. 10–11% domestic. Listed on NSE/BSE August 2025 via ₹650 Cr IPO (₹275 Cr fresh issue + ₹375 Cr OFS).

This is an asset-light-to-moderate compounder with genuine pricing power in exports (AISC/CWB moat) but commodity pass-through risk on domestic side. Revenue is project-based; 54% repeat customer rate and growing export mix provide stability. Key drivers: order inflows, Cheyyar utilisation ramp (23% → 60% expected by FY28), and export mix expansion (13% → 20%+ of revenue) which structurally lifts blended EBITDA margins.

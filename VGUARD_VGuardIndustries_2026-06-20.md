## Business Primer

V-Guard is a branded consumer electricals company with products spanning stabilizers, wires & cables, fans, switchgear, water heaters, and pumps. The business has a genuine narrow moat — dominant brand loyalty in South India (35–40% stabilizer share, deep distribution in Kerala/Tamil Nadu/Andhra Pradesh) — but is only a challenger brand nationally. Approximately 50–60% of manufacturing is outsourced, making it an asset-light, capital-efficient model.

**Key revenue streams:** Electrical/Electronics (~60%), Wires & Cables (~25%), Consumer Durables (~15%).
